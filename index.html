FAQ

Questions, Answered.

How much does TV mounting cost in Honolulu?

Most TV mounts fall in a predictable range based on TV size, wall type, and whether you need cables hidden in-wall. Call (808) 201-1311 or fill the form and we'll give you a free flat-rate quote up front — no surprises.

Can you mount on concrete or brick walls?

Yes. We bring masonry bits and the proper anchors rated for the weight of your TV. Concrete is one of our specialties on Oahu.

Can you hide the cables in the wall?

In most cases yes — we'll inspect the wall for studs, fire blocks, and outlets first. If in-wall isn't possible, we use a clean paintable raceway so nothing dangles.

What TV sizes can you mount?

Anything from a 32" bedroom flat-panel to an 85"+ living-room TV, and multi-screen video walls for offices.

Do I need to provide the mount?

If you already bought one, perfect. If not, tell us your TV size and we'll recommend the right mount for your wall and viewing position.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes, when our schedule allows — and it usually does. Call us early in the day for the best chance at same-day.
